Knitted Tank Top (Striped Top)

    Knitted summer of 2016, made with Yarn Co., 50% cotton, 50% acrylic, will fit a 42’-44’ bust.  I designed this tank myself to wear for the summer. It has a nice loose fit and drape which is nice for the hot summer weather.

    The textured seed stitch works up nice in the strip pattern.  The yarn was dyed to stripe in that color sequence which makes it fun to knit with.  I like to wear it with some capris and a cute pair of sandals.
