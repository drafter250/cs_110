Welcome to KnittyGirlDesigns

Hi, I’m Tyna also known as Knittygirl. I’m a stay-at-home mom of two boys. I am crazy about yarn and knitting (and crochet, too)!! I taught myself how to knit about 12 years ago while I was deployed to Iraq. I worked as a combat medic in the Army and during my down time in the hospital I worked in, I would knit. It was a great help to deal with all the difficult things I had to see while I was there.


I learned to crochet from my grandmother who made the most amazing crochet blankets. She taught me the basics and I learned other skills on my own. Please check out my site to learn new skills, buy my knit and crochet items, and increase your joy of knitting and crochet.


I have a number of my most favorite projects for sale in my Store I also work on commission when time permits. Take a look at My Portfolio and Store. If there is something you like in my Portfolio that is not in the Store please use the custom request form and I will get back to you with my availability.
